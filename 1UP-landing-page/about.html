The Team


Whenever people come together, at work, in friendship groups, family, or other activities, an atmosphere is created. There is something that it feels like to be part of that relationship or team. Often, we don’t talk about this, or at least if we do it is only when the atmosphere has become sour, unpleasant, or toxic. This is a difficult time to talk about how we would prefer it to be. Creating a new team is an opportunity for us to decide intentionally and in advance what atmosphere we would like to create.
