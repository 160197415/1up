The team


Whenever people come together, at work, in friendship groups, family, or other activities, an atmosphere is created. There is something that it feels like to be part of that relationship or team. Often, we don’t talk about this, or at least if we do it is only when the atmosphere has become sour, unpleasant, or toxic. This is a difficult time to talk about how we would prefer it to be. Creating a new team is an opportunity for us to decide intentionally and in advance what atmosphere we would like to create. In your teams, you will be working together on a project for several months. What do you want your experience of this team’s atmosphere to feel like? Agree as a team, and write down as many words here as you can, that describe what your team wants its atmosphere to feel like.

  • Thinh Tran -Software developer/ Business Analyst

    Having had the chance to work, after graduated, it’s a surprise to see the lack of software automation in many workplaces, outside the tech companies. In fact, people are just not very good with using software in general. It is unclear whether the applications are difficult to use or people’s refusal to change, that lead to managements using primitive systems or books to handle data. However, the benefit of software automation is real, I have seen tools that were developed from Excel, that can compile information from other systems to speed up the processes. But companies do not usually have many Software Developer within their organisation structure. My hope is to join these firms, or simply create more accessible tools that could support them and really accelerate the economy.
